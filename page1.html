Who we Are

With deep love for God, the universal Church and the entire human race, the Missionary Companions of St. Dunstan (Dunstan missionaries), was founded on the 19th of May, 2012 by Rev. Fr. Chukwuma J. Okafor CP, a Nigerian and Passionist Priest ordained in Rome; as a religious family of people who have the burning zeal of love, passion, affection and tenderness towards the up-keep of the mentally disordered in the society. On 14 June, 2016, the Association was recognized by Most Rev. Dr. Vincent Sowah Boi-Nai, SVD, the Catholic Bishop of Yendi Diocese, Ghana and our Principal Bishop as a Public Association to be given a Diocesan Right.
.

OUR MISSION 

The mission of the Congregation takes from the divine mission which Christ gave the Church and in union with the teachings of the Church, which is the evangelization and conversion of all peoples, but most concretely for us in particular the love we show to the mentally impaired

VISION                                         

The vision of the Congregation is the realization of the mission through evangelizing and rehabilitation of a good number of the mentally impaired.

The Charism

The Charism of the Congregation is the gift of going for the mentally impaired. To have an affectionate feeling and sympathy for these brethren of ours who are helpless, roaming the streets and the market places and feeding from the dustbins.

The Spirit

The Spirit of the Congregation lies in the fact that the dignity of every human person which has been destroyed through mental illness can be restored to its original image of God
