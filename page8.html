Our Core Duties

StoreM4

The Apostolate of the Congregation is to take care of the mentally impaired everywhere irrespective of their origin and background. We offer them serious rehabilitation geared towards reintegration into their families and society.
However, those who have been rehabilitated after spending a long period of time with us and who cannot go back to their families become a part of our community of brethren by staying permanently with us.
 
Presently, we have eleven (11) Priests, and about fifteen (15) Seminarians of different classes in formation. It is not quite an easy task, but we have our trust in the Lord, whose love has called us to undertake such a unique mission. We are hopeful that the recovered ones will be successfully reintegrated into the society, since they can now defend and work for themselves and their families.
